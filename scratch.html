Growing up, if I wasn't in school I was outside with a ball. That never really changed.

Why I Built This Site

Growing up, all I did was play sports. Basketball in the driveway, football in the backyard, baseball at the park — didn't matter what, as long as there was a ball and competition. There's nothing like being outside with your friends, keeping score, and trying to win.

That love never went away. Now when I'm bored I'm either on ESPN refreshing scores, or scrolling Instagram — it's already full of highlights, trade rumors, and clips. Sports take over your whole feed and I'm fine with that.

So I built Sports Hub — play games, make brackets, see stats, watch highlights, check scores. Everything a sports fan wants. Click Sports Hub ↗ in the nav to go there.
